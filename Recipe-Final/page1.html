Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 3/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips
